OUR BEGINNING

Best Pharmacy & Clinics provide quality and affordable healthcare services right in your neighborhood. Whether it is questions about your medication or prescription management, our services will make your life just a little easier.

"At Best Pharmacy & Clinics our aim is to redefine the healthcare space by providing you with affordable clinical services and discounted pharmaceuticals and over-the-counter health and hygiene products all under one roof."

Our Best Pharmacy & Clinics have in-house qualified medical doctors dispensing prescription drugs and providing diagnostic services for a nominal consulting fee.

As part of our social obligations we provide free emergency house calls and routine house calls for registered users who are aged and ailing, particularly those above 70 years.

With these and many more health services, Best Pharmacy & Clinic aims to bring hassle-free healthcare closer to middle class neighborhoods.
